What is Cards for Humanity?

Cards for Humanity is a fast-paced online version of the card game
Cards for Humanity that gives you the opputunity to donate to children in need
All the while remaining as despicable as you naturally are



Warning: This game contains offensive, profane, explicit content created by cards against humanity
and provided here, uncesnsored and complete, in digital form. Human Resource told us to say that.

CHARITY

"If you don't give back, no one will like you ™" - crowdrise.com"

We are quite sure you feel the same way so we've integrated donation widget (via crowdrise.com), along with some not-so-subtle
reminders throughout the game to give you an opportunity to donate. If you're singed in when you donate, we'll even upgrade
your account and place a crown on you avatar's head ( to remind other players how much better you are than them ).
All donations go to the Make-A-Wish-Foundation and benefit children in need
